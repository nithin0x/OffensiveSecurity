# AI
This folder contains research notes, scripts, and tooling related to AI security, prompt-engineering risks, and model misuse analysis. Content may include safe demonstrations, defensive checks, and guidance for secure prompt and model handling.

## Safety & Legal
AI research can expose sensitive data or enable harmful capabilities. Do not publish or run experiments that expose private data or that could be used to cause harm. Follow responsible disclosure and research ethics.

## Contributing
When adding examples, include the dataset used (or a sanitized sample), the model or framework version, and intended scope of the test.
